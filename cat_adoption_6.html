Meet Georgie!

Female, Housetrained

Georgie, or "baby shark" as her caregivers fondly refer to her as, is a senior gal who is full of life and wants nothing more than to be in your lap. She is curious about all her surroundings, loves to go on stroller and harness walks and is dog tested. With those big green eyes she will put you in a trance when you look into them so she usually gets what she wants. She receives insulin twice a day for diabetes but don't let that scare you, we can show you how to administer it. Will you be the one lucky enough to give this girl a home?
