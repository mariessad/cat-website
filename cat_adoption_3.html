Meet Royal Fluffington!

Male, Housetrained

Royal Fluffington is a regal elder cat who will add an air of class and sophistication to your home. Although very shy at first, with patience and time RF will turn into a loving ball of purrs. If you approach him slowly you'll get to see his true, sweet personality shine.

RF would do well in a quiet home where he can come out of his shell with plenty of time, patience, and loving subjects.
