Meet Flapjack!

Female, Housetrained

Flapjack is a sassy young cat who makes her own fun. She always finds something to play with and especially loves chasing feather-on-a-string toys. Although she likes to have her own space, she gets along with other cats. Born in 2011, Slidell can't use her back legs, but they don't cause her any pain or slow her down. She's FIV and incontinent, so needs to have her bladder expressed and take some medications. During her daily baths, she grumbles and growls while planning her escape, but she's all talk.
